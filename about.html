About Me

Hey there, I'm Yash Pal, a passionate artist on a journey of self-discovery through the world of art. Self-taught and driven by my own curiosity, I've delved into the captivating realms of anime art and sketches, finding joy and expression in every stroke of the pencil. As a beginner, I'm constantly honing my skills and exploring new techniques, often turning to the vast resources of YouTube for inspiration and guidance. Alongside my artistic pursuits, I'm currently pursuing my degree in Information Technology, finding the perfect balance between the worlds of creativity and technology. Join me as I continue to learn, create, and share my artistic journey with the world.
